1) [Background image: Three bacon egg muffins on a white plate with a silver fork]

Narrator: "Start by preheating your oven to 350 degrees. Generously grease a muffin tin with butter to prevent sticking."

2) [Background image: Bacon being whisked with chives, cheese and eggs]

Narrator: "In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, cooked bacon, green onions, 1 cup cheese, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Set the mixture aside for the next step."

3) [Background image: Egg mixture being poured into the muffin tin]

Narrator: "Carefully pour the egg mixture into each muffin cavity, filling them 3/4 of the way full. Sprinkle the remaining cheddar cheese on top for extra flavor."

4) [Background image: Bacon egg muffins in a muffin tin ready to be baked]

Narrator: "Bake the muffins for 15-20 minutes until they look set and slightly puffed up. Let them cool for 2-3 minutes before serving. Enjoy your homemade Bacon Egg Muffins!"
